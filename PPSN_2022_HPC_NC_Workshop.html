Abstract:

Natural Computing is ideal for High-Performance Computing workloads, mainly because of the inherent parallelism. Traditionally, natural computing methods have been designed for CPU-based machines. With the decline in Mooreâ€™s law, there is an increasing interest and need to port the CPU-based algorithms to GPUs and other high-performance platforms. The porting of these algorithms is not a mere software-engineering activity. The porting involves several modifications to the original algorithm. This workshop aims to bring awareness on the importance of this topic, and help bridge the gap between systems and algorithms groups.
